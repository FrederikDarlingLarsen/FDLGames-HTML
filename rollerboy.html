Below is a demo of the upcoming game "RollerBoy". This demo lets you play through the first world of the game in arcade mode only. The final version of the game will have six different worlds exploring different genres along with a very special final world. All these worlds can then be experienced as part of a coherent story in story mode or individually in order to hunt achievements in arcade mode.

The game window is powered by WebGL which means that your browser must support this.
