About me

Hi, my name is Frederik Darling and i make games. I have been making games for more than 10 years with my first game being made in GameMaker, before i knew anything about programming or any game design theory. Most of those games were not very good.

I was very fortunate to attend a technical highschoole from 2015-2018 where Game Design was the focus. This meant that i was both prepared for higher education in general while learning a bit about making games. This was when i started To familirize myself with the Unity game engine, and slowly learned to code along the way. I was not very good at coding in the beginning but i got better when i entered university.

When i studied medialogy from 2019-2022 at Aalborg University in Copenhagen, i improved my coding skills significantly and the semester projects very a significant improvement from other proects i had worked on before.
